About Our Classes

Our esteemed Acharyas conduct regular classes and workshops on Vedanta, meditation, and spiritual practices. These sessions are designed to help seekers understand the profound wisdom of our ancient scriptures and apply them in daily life.

How to Join

  • In-Person Classes: Please arrive 15 minutes before the scheduled time. No prior registration required unless specified.
  • Online Classes: Registration links are provided in the event description. You will receive a confirmation email with joining instructions.

Class Schedule Updates

This calendar is regularly updated with new classes and events. You can subscribe to our newsletter to receive notifications about upcoming sessions.
